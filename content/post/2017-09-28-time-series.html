These are the pakages we will need to make the intended time series plot;

library(Lahman)
library(sqldf)
library(ggplot2)

Now, say we wanted to see progessive change over time- we would contruct a time series graph. In this case we wanted to see Babe Ruths number of homeruns over the years; Note- time is always on the x axis and the changing varaible will be on the y axis Extracting the data;

query<-"SELECT yearID,HR
FROM Batting
WHERE playerID='ruthba01'"

result<-sqldf(query)

Now, letâ€™s visualize this;

ggplot()+
  geom_line(data=result,aes(x=yearID,y=HR))+
  geom_point(data=result,aes(x=yearID,y=HR))+
  ggtitle("Ruth's Homerun Total Through the Years")+
  xlab("year")+
  ylab("homeruns")
